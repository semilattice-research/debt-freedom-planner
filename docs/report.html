Study 1 · Discovery

User Research · understand_users · UK Adults 2026 – Consumer Finance · n=804 · sim 4d51bac5

46.6%Budgeting is the top frustration — five times the next problem (debt payoff, 10.0%).
39.7%Biggest complaint about existing apps: "no useful advice — just data."
93.8%Prefer or insist that a tool does not connect to their bank.
85.5%Would abandon a tool that shares data with third parties — the single strongest signal in the study.
22.8%Trust a tool that explains how it makes money (the top driver, FCA approval at 39.3%, is unclaimable for us).
∴

The opportunity is not a better dashboard. It is a tool that takes your numbers, tells you exactly what to do — and visibly refuses to take your data.
